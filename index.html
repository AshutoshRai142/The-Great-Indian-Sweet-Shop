Gulab Jamun


Ingridents

  • Milk Powder / Khoya
  • Maida
  • Baking Powder
  • Ghee

Recipe

  1. Combine sugar and water in a pan and bring to a boil to create the syrup
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine flour, paneer, sooji, Nestlé MILKMAID, baking powder, and baking soda
  3. Divide the dough into 30-35 portions and gently shape them into round balls.

Kaju Katli

Ingridents

  • Kashew Nuts
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Ghee

Recipe

  1. Grind all the Cashew Nuts properly
  2. Make the sweet syrup by mixing water and sugar.
  3. Now mix the sweet syrup with the grinded cashew nuts and mix it properly then give the shape of kaju katli and put in fridge for some time
